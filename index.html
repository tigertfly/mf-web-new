Welcome to MobiFlight Project

Empowering your flight simulation experience!

MobiFlight integrates standard Arduino hardware with your flight simulator - this allows you to build your individual cockpit in a fast, cheap and flexible way! MobiFlight is also free and open source.

Download MobiFlight

Main Features

MobiFlight integrates with popular flight simulators such as Microsoft Flight Simulator, X-Plane, and Prepar3D.

MobiFlight supports popular microcontroller boards, including Arduino Mega, Pro Micro, Nano, and the Raspberry Pi RP2040 "Pico", allowing users to build versatile setups with off the shelf hardware.

MobiFlight comes with a predefined firmware that lets you assign your input and output devices to simulator functions without programming. This makes MobiFlight accessible and useful to both beginners and advanced users.
